Homelessness In Oakland

Info:

  • According to EveryOne Counts, as of February 2022, there are 5,055 homeless residents in Oakland

    • Of the 5,055 Oakland homeless residents, 1,718 are sheltered and 3,337 unsheltered.

    • 32% of unsheltered individuals were observed in tents, 31% in a car or van, 27% in an RV, and 10% on the street or in abandoned builings.

  • 47% of unsheltered individuals in Alameda County Live in Oakland

    • 2,612 individuals in Alameda County are enrolled in sheltered services.

    • 7,135 individuals in Alameda County were observed as being unsheltered.
