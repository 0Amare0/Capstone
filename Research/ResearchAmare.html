Homelessness in Oakland

How can we help people avoid homelessness in Oakland?

Key Findings:

Initial Research

With Oakland's homeless population having dramatically increased over the past few years, the city has been unable provide nearly enough affordable housing. The city lacks the data and resources to adequaly handle the crisis.

Root Causes

Homelessness often occurs when multiple personal and societal factors co-present. A personal hardship such as addiction, grief, or any other circumstances hindering someones ability to properly function can cause someone to falter in life and without a sufficient social safety net they fall into homelessness.

Case Study

My case study has shown that an app using cognitive behavioral therapy can help addiction patients function better and lessen the negative effects addiction can have in their lives. An app that is more widely available to provide mental health aid and help improve ilfe skills could hepl people avoid falling into a downward spiral.
